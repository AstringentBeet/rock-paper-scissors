Rock, Paper, Scissors

You know the rules: rock beats scissors, scissors slices paper, and paper covers rock. A full on cycle, yo.

This game works directly with the alert box (the annoying box that occasionally declares you the winner of something random like a Walmart gift card of $25,000.00 or a sprouted grain everything bagel with avocado spread).

Once you're gassed up and ready to throw hands in the biggest showdown of the century, go ahead and toggle "Begin Game" to, well, begin the game conssisting of 5 rounds. Press "Start Over" to restart the scoreboard. Type "yes" or "no", "rock", "paper", or "scissors" when prompted.

Good Luck!
